Our Story

Two Decades of Building Champaign

Gower Construction was founded in 2005 by Preston James Dean Gower with a simple mission — deliver honest, high-quality construction work to the families and businesses of Champaign-Urbana. What began as a small residential contracting operation has grown into one of the area's most trusted construction companies.

Over the years, we've built hundreds of homes, renovated countless commercial spaces, and earned a reputation for doing things the right way — on time, on budget, and with craftsmanship that stands the test of time. Every project, big or small, receives the same level of dedication and attention to detail.

We're proud to be a local Champaign company. Our team lives here, our families are here, and we're invested in making this community a better place — one build at a time.

How We Work

Our Construction Process

We've refined our process over 20 years to make every project smooth, transparent, and stress-free for our clients.

01

Free Consultation

We meet at your site, listen to your goals, assess the scope, and discuss options that fit your needs and budget.

02

Written Estimate

You receive a detailed, itemized written estimate. No hidden fees, no vague language — just clear numbers.

03

Scheduled Build

We lock in your timeline, pull permits, coordinate materials, and get to work with regular progress updates.

04

Final Handover

We walk the project with you, address any final items, and only close out when you're fully satisfied.
